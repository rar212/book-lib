HAPPY BIRTHDAYYYYYYY LOSERRR, I have not written one of these in a minute but I would like to wish u a very frickin happy birthday!!!! I know that right now you are going through a lot but InshaAllah with due time things will be much better for you. The world is a cruel place but with people like you in it, it makes it this much better. I think about you every single day and I pray that you feel at peace with everything despite everything that's going on. Sometimes I take you for granted but I honestly can't live my life without you. You're my favourite -- no scratch that, you are MY PERSON and you always will be no matter how little we talk or interact every day. I don't usually put 110% effort when it comes to things but with you I always try my absolute best to keep you at peace and at comfort. I pray that things work out (please please). Our time apart just feels like an eternity, and every passing day only deepened the ache in my soul. Yet, in the midst of the longing, I held on to the memories we shared, it's almost like a treasure that keeps me going and my spirit alive. I will always cherish the lessons we're learning during our time apart, for they have shown us the value of what we have. We have grown, we have yearned, and we have discovered that inner peace is the best remedy for all of our problems. I also lied the other day when I said I don't want to be dependent because trust me there's nothing more in this world, I'd want other than to share things with you but I will save it for when the time is right InshaAllah. I hope you have a good day and I hope this message makes your day just a tad bit better.


(PS: Sometimes saying a phrase doesn't do it justice, so I won't end with it, but I hope this makes it clear for you that it's always true, and always will be ml)
